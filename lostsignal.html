Lost Signal
Lost Signal is a puzzle platformer in which you play as a little robot on your first day on the job as a satellite repair bot. Lost Signal was a game jam submission with the theme of "lost signal." I did the programming, art, SFX, and playtesting for the game while a friend of mine did the music. Lost Signal can be played as a web export on my itch.io page here.
