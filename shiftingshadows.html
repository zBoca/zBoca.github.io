Shifting Shadows
Shifting Shadows is an alchemical puzzle game where you use your powers of transmutation, transformation, and Shadow Flipping in order to get to the end of a series of stages. Shifting Shadows was a game jam submission in which the theme was "Shadows and Alchemy." I did the programming, art, some playtesting, and created a Game Design Document, while 2 friends of mine helped with music, some of the level designs, and playtesting. Shifting Shadows as well as a link to the Game Design Document can be found on my itch.io page here.
