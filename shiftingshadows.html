Shifting Shadows
Shifting Shadows is an alchemical puzzle game. Shifting Shadows was a game jam submission in which the theme was "Shadows and Alchemy." I did the programming, art, playtesting, and created a Game Design Document, while 2 friends of mine helped with music, some of the level designs, and playtesting.
