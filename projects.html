    Lost Signal

    Lost Signal is a small game in which you embark on a journey as a cute little robot on his first day on the job as a Satellite Repair Robot. You journey out to malfunctioning satellites and recievers to fix them in an effort to restore the Lost Signal.

    Lost Signal was made for the Clover Jam 4 Game Jam hosted on itch.io, and is a relatively small game consisting of only 5 levels. The jam lasted for two weeks, and I participated with my spare time between going to school and my job. I coded the whole project, as well as making the assets and sound effects, however the music was made by a friend of mine. This was the first jam I had ever participated in, and the first game that I had ever finished and released. All of the code for the game is public on my github page, which can be found here, or you can try it out on itch.io here.
