    • AntiVirus


    AntiVirus is a fast-paced pixel-art tower defense game in which you deploy your Antiviruses to stop the waves of invading computer viruses from reaching your CPU.

    This game has spawning waves of enemies that follow a path, and dynamically placeable towers that will find the Virus that is closest to the CPU before doing actions that are unique to each tower.

    AntiVirus was fully released to Steam on August 25th, 2025.
