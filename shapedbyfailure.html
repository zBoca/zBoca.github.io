Shaped By Failure
Shaped by Failure is a top down reversed take on the rogue-like genre and a bullethell. Shaped by Failure was a submission for a "Bullet Hell" themed game jam. I did the programming, art, and playtesting, and friends of mine worked on the music, playtesting, and helped with enemy designs. Shaped by Failure can be played as a web export on my itch.io page here.
